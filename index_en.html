ABOUT US

Established in May 1973 and listed on the KOSDAQ market in 2001, Sungho Electronics primarily focuses on manufacturing and sales of film capacitors, which serve as vital components of passive circuit configuration for power supply units (PSU), and electric electronic finished products. As seen its selection as the Ministry of Knowledge Economy's World Class 300, SME with Managerial Innovation (MAIN-BIZ), and SME with Technical Innovation (INNO-BIZ), the company strives to facilitate continued growth.
